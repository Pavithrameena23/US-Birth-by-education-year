ğŸ“Œ Project Overview
This project examines the shifting landscape of United States births over a pivotal six-year period. Using Tableau, I processed multi-year census-style data to uncover patterns in birth rates, maternal demographics, and regional variances. The dashboard provides a clear visualization of how national birth trends have evolved, offering insights into population growth and healthcare planning requirements.

ğŸ“Š Key Insights
Temporal Trends: Identified year-over-year fluctuations in birth rates, highlighting the specific impact of socio-economic factors on family planning between 2016 and 2021.

Maternal Demographics: Analyzed birth distribution across different maternal age groups, identifying shifts toward later-in-life parenthood trends.

Regional Hotspots: Mapped birth density across US states to pinpoint regions with the highest growth, essential for infrastructure and school district planning.

Birth Characteristics: Explored data related to birth settings and methods, providing a lens into the modern American healthcare experience.
